~TECH Index~
Techniques and classes are what define a character's abilities beyond their generic traits. Techniques are gained from a character's Classes. A character gains one Technique for every rank they have in a Tech Class.
All tech classes currently available are detailed below.
~


All Classes colored blue are considered Skills, while those colored purple are considered Insights.
Combatant
1st G
Wisdom
Dexterity
Speed
     A person's familiarity with the application of violence. A necessary class for any aspiring fighter.
Class Bonus: This class' Rank can be added to Grapple checks, and used for advantage when beginning combat under special conditions.
Special EXP: Survive Battles.
❶. Jab
Access to power level 1 attacks, which deal half of standard damage, and require no recovery moments.
:: Cost: 1 SKL
❷.Guard
Protect yourself by placing an object or limb ahead of your vulnerabilities. Guard differs from block in that it can be focused.
:: Cost: 1 SKL
❸.Feignt
A false attack that triggers any stances currently active on the target without commiting to an attack. Leaving you free to counter the destabilized opponent.
:: Cost: P
❹.Fletch
A dashing attack that covers as many spaces as the character has Speed. The attack may be swapped for any action.
:: Cost: 1 SKL
❺.Riposte
An action taken in the recovery moments after another attack action. If the prior attack parries, this technique is triggered, imediately launching into another attack and canceling the previous attack's recovery.
:: Cost: 1 SKL
❻.Haymaker
grants access to level 5 attacks, which deal double standard damage, and require 4 recovery moments.
:: Cost: 1 SKL
Commander
3rd G
Combatant
Charisma
Tactician
     A leader of subordinates. One who can affectively communicate in the heat of chaos, to coordinate groups into more effective tactics.
Class Bonus: A Commander check may be performed to gain a summary of the current enemy force's next moves.
Special EXP: Give successful orders in combat.
⤷❶. Call-out
Communicate without consuming an action.
:: Cost: 1 SKL
⤷❷.Coordinate
Speaking for the round no longer consumes actions.
:: Cost: 1 SKL
⤷❸.Order
Communication has no cost in battle, and other characters gain advantage when acting on your instruction.
:: Cost: P
❹.Inspire
All friendly characters able to hear your your order, gain +1 Spd for the round.
:: Cost: 1 SKL
❺.Command
All friendly characters able to hear your your order, gain +1 Dex for the round.
:: Cost: 1 SKL
❻.Rouse
All friendly characters able to hear your order will have their next strike deal double damage. (Does not affect range damage, but will double the potential draw weight usable by archers.)
:: Cost: 1 SKL
