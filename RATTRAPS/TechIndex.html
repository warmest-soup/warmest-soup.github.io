     These techniques are available to all characters. They are fundemental, and often very beneficial to characters that have vulnerabilities, or gaps in their build.
Class Bonus: N/A
Special EXP: N/A
❶. Block
The act of protecting something, by placing something else in the way. Characters are able to defend vulnerabile limbs, and companions, by placing equipped objects, or one's own limbs in the way of oncoming strikes. The strike will then target the item using the same target-roll used by the attacker.
:: Cost: Free
❷.Scramble
The ability to throw one's self away from a threat, to a distance equal to the result of a single dice roll. The acting character is left prone. This ability is evasive, and thus can be used even while destabilized.
:: Cost: 1
❸.Throw
The act of launching an object, item, or character with one's hands. This Technique may act as a ranged attack, or as a way to deliver the thrown object to a companion. This Technique is performed as one does any ranged attack. The thrown item MUST have less weight than the acting character has strength.
:: Cost: 1
❹.Item Smash
The act of intentionally attempting to break an item. This technique may act as an attack, or simply to break a held item. The Character must roll an athleticism check; If successful, the item will break. broken items deal damage equal to double their DT.
:: Cost: 1
❺.Shoulder
Any character may offer aid to a wounded companion in the event that they are immobile. Characters shouldering a companion will be able to move with their companion at their natural rate.
:: Cost: Free
❻.Desperation
A single use buff to either garuntee success in a check, or double damage. This technique may be used only once per battle.
:: Cost: 2
