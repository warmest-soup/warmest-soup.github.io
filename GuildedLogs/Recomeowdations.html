I don't read horror often, but Bibliomania has intrigue and mystery in spades, with a main character that's just as fascinating as the horrors she encounters and the delusions of the people she meets along the way.

The art is great too. It really sells the whole "eldritch" vibe.

If you're going to read anything on this list, let it be Bibliomania. It's one of the most unique manga I've ever read, and it's short enough that you can finish it in one sitting too.
